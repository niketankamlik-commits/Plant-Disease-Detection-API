About The Model

Our backend inference engine is powered by an EfficientNet Convolutional Neural Network.

  • Capable of identifying 39 distinct plant/disease combinations.
  • Processes imagery internally at 160x160 resolution.
  • Uses deep feature extraction for highly accurate confidence scoring.

Best Practices

For the model to accurately detect early signs of blight, rust, and pests, please adhere to the following:

  • Take a clear, close-up photo of the affected Leaf.
  • Do NOT include the fruit or stalk of the plant in the frame.
  • Ensure the background behind the leaf is as plain or solid as possible (e.g. against a piece of paper or clear ground).

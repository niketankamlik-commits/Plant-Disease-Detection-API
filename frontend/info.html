Scanning Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to get the most accurate results from our AI model.

Model Info

Our model is trained on 38 different classes of plant diseases across 14 species. It performs best on clear, high-resolution leaf images.

Lighting

Ensure the leaf is well-lit. Natural daylight is best. Avoid harsh shadows or extremely bright direct sunlight that washes out details.

Focus & Framing

  • Center the affected part of the leaf.
  • Ensure the image is in sharp focus.
  • Avoid backgrounds with other plants.

Quality Check

If the confidence score is below 70%, we recommend retaking the photo with better lighting or focus.
